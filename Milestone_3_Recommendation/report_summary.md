 Student Cluster Analysis & Recommendation Summary

**Dataset Overview**

* **Total Students:** 480
* **Clusters Identified:** 3
* **Engagement Metrics Used:**

  * `raisedhands` (class participation)
  * `VisITedResources` (online activity)
  * `AnnouncementsView` (attention to updates)
  * `Discussion` (collaborative learning)

**Cluster Insights & Behavior Patterns**

**Cluster 0**

* *Engagement:* Moderate class participation and fair resource use
* *Behavioral Insight:* Students show steady effort but need structure to maintain consistency.
* *Recommendation:* Keep your routine; try peer tutoring to enhance understanding.

**Cluster 1**

* *Engagement:* High participation and strong resource engagement
* *Behavioral Insight:* Highly motivated, consistent learners performing well academically.
* *Recommendation:* Study with focus; reduce absences; maintain progress with advanced material.

**Cluster 2**

* *Engagement:* Low participation but higher discussion activity
* *Behavioral Insight:* Social learners who engage verbally but need structured study and follow-up.
* *Recommendation:* Review weekly; use digital flashcards to reinforce learning.

**Key Observations**

* **Cluster 1 (~40%)** students are high performers and should receive enrichment opportunities.
* **Cluster 0 (~35%)** students need consistent guidance to sustain engagement.
* **Cluster 2 (~25%)** students benefit from structured accountability and short review cycles.
